Research Project

Research in Progress Lead Researcher: Aashiga Moorthy

The Impact of AI Chatbot Use on Teen Loneliness and Emotional Well-Being

Research Question

How do frequent interactions with anthropomorphic AI chatbots influence reported feelings of loneliness, emotional attachment, and real-world social interaction among high school adolescents?

Motivation

As conversational AI companions become widely accessible, adolescents increasingly turn to synthetic agents for advice and emotional expression. Understanding the psychological balance between immediate comfort and long-term peer engagement is critical for ethical AI development.

Background & Methodology

Combining qualitative survey analysis, adapted psychological loneliness scales, and interaction pattern analysis to evaluate how conversational AI usage correlates with student well-being metrics.

Current Progress & Next Steps

Data synthesis and qualitative categorization are currently underway. The goal is to establish design guidelines that promote healthy boundaries in conversational interfaces.
