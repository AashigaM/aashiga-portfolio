About Me

Biography

I am a rising high school senior based in California with a deep interest in computer science, machine learning, and human-centered technology.

My journey into computer science stems from a curiosity about how software systems scale and how intelligent models can be leveraged to address complex social problems. Over the past few years, I have supplemented my high school education with advanced collegiate dual enrollment coursework in computer science and web development.

Beyond traditional coursework, my experience includes conducting independent academic research on the psychological impacts of conversational AI, building interactive simulation systems, and founding programming workshops to teach introductory coding to younger students in my community.

Academic Milestones & Initiatives

  • Dual Enrollment Coursework: Completed rigorous college-level computer science and development classes alongside high school curriculum.
  • Independent AI Research: Authoring an academic paper investigating the influence of conversational chatbots on teen emotional health.
  • Community Education: Founded and hosted coding workshops focused on foundational programming concepts and web engineering.
  • Full-Stack & Web Projects: Developed web applications ranging from interactive physics and astronomy simulators to real-time environmental API dashboards.
